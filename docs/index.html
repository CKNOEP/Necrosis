About Necrosis

Necrosis is the ultimate World of Warcraft addon for Warlocks, providing comprehensive UI management for soul shards, spells, demon summoning, buffs, curses, and timers. Built with passion and refined through over 20 years of continuous development and community support.

This addon features an innovative radial button interface, graphical soul shard display, extensive customization options, and advanced features like Summon Queue management with raid synchronization. Whether you're a casual Warlock or a hardcore raider, Necrosis has you covered.
